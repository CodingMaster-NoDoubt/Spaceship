How to play

  • You can use the arrows "left" and "right" to move the spaceship. To shoot, press space.
  • You will notice that there are balls of three different sizes. The bigger they are, the more balls it takes to kill them and the more points they give (10, 30, 50).
  • These balls are likely to give you a temporary power that will make your task easier. The more powerful the power, the less time it lasts.
  • Every second your score increases by one point and your best score will be recorded.
  • You can pause the game at any time and restart a game if you want to.
  • Enjoy !
